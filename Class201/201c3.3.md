# Lists  

**HTML lists:** *ordered, unordered, and definition*  
- Ordered lists: numbers  
  - `<ol, </ol>` and `<li>, </li>`
- Unordered lists: bullets  
  - `<ul>, </ul>` and `<li>, </li>`
- Definitions lists: define terminology  
  - `<dl>, </dl>` and `<dt>, </dt>` and `<dd>, </dd>`

*Lists can be nested inside on another
